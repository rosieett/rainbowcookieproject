The Perfect Cookie

While elusive, the perfect rainbow cookie is a delightful mix of almond-flavored cake, thin layers of raspberry jam, and a dark chocolate coating. The are the quintessential New York Italian-American dessert, largely only available in NYC and neighboring New York towns. The cake layers are colored to align to the Italian flag, but sometimes vary for different occasions.

What makes up a rainbow cookie?

1
The Chocolate
Tasty, smooth, adds to the cookie, perfect ratio. Shouldn't be too sweet.
2
The Almond Flavor
Perfect almond taste, not liquor-y or extracty
3
The Jam
At least raspberry, sometimes apricot as well, not too much that it drips from the cookie.
4
The Cake Layers
layers are similar in height for each layer with a tight crumb, moist but not wet
5
The Color
bright and artificial, but not so much food coloring you can taste it

May 30, 2024: I googled “bakery” within the bounds of south: 36th ave; west: the east river, but most west was Cresent; north: the end of Queens but the most north was Ditmars Ave; east: 51st street.

June 1, 2024: I walked 5.24 miles, stopping at every bakery on the list (below) and purchased “two rainbow cookies.” I marked the bakery bag with a number, corresponding to the list of bakeries I created to remember which cookie came from which bakery, noted the cost, and kept walking to the next.

It took me 2.5 hours and $37.55.

I then weighed and photographed each set of cookies, and assigned new numbers so I could also be tasting them as blind as the other tasters.

I invited my sister, Mattie, my friend Tracy, and my husband, Ken, to be the first round of blind taste testers. Each cookie sat next to it’s assigned kind-of-random number between 1 and 13.

They were cut into smaller pieces and as a group, we went one cookie at a time. A piece was eaten and the scores were documented.

There was some discussion but overall, we generally kept our thoughts to the score card.

There was also saltines, carrots and celery, and unsalted slivered almonds for palette clearing.

Upon completion, I then went through and shared the locations for each cookie.

Scoring Rubric
Almond no almond or marzipan vibes or so much that it ruins the cookie bad extract or liquor amount choices essence of almond but that’s about it can taste the almond, meets expectations great almond taste, not extract-y or liquor-y
Chocolate gross choices for chocolate have been made chocolate is bad ratio is off, chocolate is chalky/wrong texture, taste is meh good but not amazing, adequate ratio perfect - tasty, smooth, adds to the cookie, perfect ratio
Texture bad. too wet or way too dry - distractingly so dryer or wetter than expectations fine - meets rainbow cookie expectations perfectly moist, crumbles, but is not dense
Jam non existent bad, not there, sad about it not noticeable or wrong ratio good but not notable, meets expectations flavorful, thin and perfect ratio

Rosie and Adri are star-crossed design soul mates who met clandestinely through working as graphics reporters on the same team at the Wall Street Journal.
