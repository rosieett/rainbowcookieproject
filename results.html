About the creators

Rosie and Adri are star-crossed design soul mates who met clandestinely through working as graphics reporters on the same team at the Wall Street Journal. They regularly match each other’s silliness - including, but not limited to making entire websites about cookies, love for pets, food, and their shared colleague-turned-bestie, Danny (who also taught Rosie d3).

Rosie is a born and raised New Yorker and loves rainbow cookies maybe a little too much. Rosie thought up the concept of the project and all that comes with it, coded it, and ate a lot of cookies. She learned through this project so if you see coding errors, feel free to let me know!

Adri has never had a rainbow cookie but loves to design them. Adri took Rosie's horrible mock ups and made it the beauty you see before you. She also figured out the UX design for the project.
